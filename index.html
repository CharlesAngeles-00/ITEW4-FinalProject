Philippine Presidents

Manuel L. Quezon

1935-1944

First president of the Commonwealth of the Philippines. Known as the "Father of the National Language" for promoting Tagalog as the national language.

Ramon Magsaysay

1953-1957

Known as the "Champion of the Masses." His administration was noted for its integrity and reforms to help the rural poor.

Ferdinand Marcos

1965-1986

Longest-serving president who declared martial law in 1972. His regime was marked by authoritarian rule and corruption.

Corazon Aquino

1986-1992

First female president of the Philippines and Asia. She restored democratic institutions after the Marcos dictatorship.
