Bongbong Marcos

2022-Present

Biography

Ferdinand "Bongbong" Romualdez Marcos Jr. is the 17th and current president of the Philippines, assuming office on June 30, 2022. He is the son of former president Ferdinand Marcos Sr. and former first lady Imelda Marcos, marking a historic political comeback for the Marcos family.

Born on September 13, 1957 in Manila, he spent his childhood in Malacañang Palace during his father's presidency and was educated in the UK and US, graduating from Oxford University (though without completing his degree). Marcos won the 2022 presidential election by landslide with 31.6 million votes, the highest in Philippine electoral history. His campaign platform centered on national unity and economic recovery post-pandemic.

Legacy

Marcos' presidency represents a remarkable political rehabilitation of the Marcos family, coming 36 years after their ouster in the 1986 People Power Revolution. His administration faces challenges including rising commodity prices, foreign debt management, and continuing pandemic recovery.

Notable early actions include appointing economic technocrats to key positions, continuing major infrastructure projects from the previous administration, and pursuing an "independent foreign policy" that balances relations with both China and the United States.

Quick Facts

  • Born: September 13, 1957
  • Age at Inauguration: 64 years
  • Political Party: Partido Federal ng Pilipinas
  • Vice President: Sara Duterte

Key Policies

  • Economic recovery through infrastructure development
  • Continued Build Build Build program
  • Focus on agricultural modernization
